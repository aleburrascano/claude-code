# Armin Ronacher

Creator of **Flask** + **Pallets Projects**; Director of Engineering at **Sentry**. Active blogger on agentic coding and AI engineering at [lucumr.pocoo.org](https://lucumr.pocoo.org/). GitHub: [@mitsuhiko](https://github.com/mitsuhiko).

For our wiki, Armin matters as a **production-grounded voice on agentic coding** — he writes from the perspective of an engineer who built and maintains widely-deployed Python infrastructure (Flask, Sentry tooling) and has spent 2025-2026 reasoning publicly about how that perspective is changing under LLM-assisted development.

## Why his perspective is high-signal

1. **Production engineer voice.** Armin maintains software with hundreds of millions of users (Flask) and runs engineering for an observability product (Sentry). The framing of "what actually breaks at scale" carries weight.
2. **Open-source maintainer perspective.** Pallets Projects style discipline (clean APIs, minimal abstractions, careful versioning) translates to how he evaluates AI-coding workflows.
3. **Skeptical-but-curious.** Doesn't oscillate between hype and doom. Practical evaluations of what works.
