## Why his perspective is high-signal

1. **Volume + consistency.** 108 posts on this tag alone. Few non-Anthropic voices match this depth on Claude Code specifically.
2. **Tool-builder, not just user.** Datasette + LLM CLI + sqlite-utils make Simon a *builder* of LLM-adjacent infrastructure. His critiques are grounded in what he's tried to ship.
3. **Independent voice.** No vendor affiliation. Willing to flag concerns (prompt injection, supply chain) that vendor blogs typically soften.
4. **Journalism-adjacent rigor.** Timestamps, links, "I tried this and here's what happened" pattern. Reproducible.
